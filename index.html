Yunpeng(Larry) Liu

I'm a fourth-year PhD student at University of British Columbia, where I'm advised by Frank Wood. I'm also an Mitacs intern at Invereted AI, where we are working on advancing autonomous driving with generative models. Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Applied Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of British Columbia.

My research focuses on autonomous driving with generative models like diffusion models, modelling realistic mult-agent behaviors and reasoning about the world.
